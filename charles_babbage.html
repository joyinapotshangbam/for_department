Tribute to Charles Babbage

About Charles Babbage

Charles Babbage was an English polymath,mathematician,philosopher,inventor and mechanical engineer,widely regarded as the pioneer of computing and often credited with originating the concept of a digital programmable computer.

Achievements

  • Designing the Difference Engine and the Analytical Engine.
  • Was a founding member of the Analytical Society.
  • Successfully broke the Vigenere autokey cipher.
  • Invented the speedometer and the locomotive cowcatcher.

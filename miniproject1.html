Classic Guacamole

This is a classic, simple guacamole recipe that is always a crowd pleaser. Perfect for parties, snacks, or as a topping for your favorite Maxican dishes. The key is using fresh, ripe ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup choped cilantro
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 small jalapeno, minced (optional)

Instructions

  1. Slice the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  2. Gently mash the avocados with a fork until you reach your desired consistency.
  3. Add the diced onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and minced jalapeno (if using).
  4. Stir everything together until well combined.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. Serve immediately.
